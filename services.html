Our Medical Services

    Life style disease clinic

    At Veda Hospital, we specialize in the prevention, early detection, and management of lifestyle-related conditions like diabetes, hypertension, obesity, and heart disease. Our team combines medical expertise with personalized care, diet counseling, and lifestyle guidance to help patients make healthier choices and prevent complications. With a focus on long-term wellness, we support you in taking control of your health and leading a balanced, active, and fulfilling life.

    24 Hour Services

    We know that your medical needs don't stop when office hours are over. Using our help line and our email service, you can get a message to your health team when it is convenient for you.

    Wellness Support

    Our team will support you in building a healthier you. No matter what your health needs are, having a team support you will keep you on the path to meeting them. We work together to connect you with the services you need.

    A Healthy Community

    When you choose us, you join a community. We work not just with you but with other members of our community to build a network of people working together for a healthier world.

    General Health

    Not feeling well or just need an annual exam? We do it all! We also perform sports physicals and referrals for injuries. Whether you're sick or only need some routine maintenance, we're here for you!

    Family Medicine

    At Veda Hospital, we treat even the smallest members of the family! From child to old, we're here for examination, diagnosis, treatment and ongoing wellness for your family.

    Critical care

    We are ready 24/7 for all medical emergencies like poisoning, heart stroke, brain stroke, respiratory illness, snake bites, unknown bites and conditions where every second matters. Our Critical care unit is equipped with state of the art equipment to cater your health care needs
